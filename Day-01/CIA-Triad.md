# CIA Triad

### Confidentiality
## Definition:
Only authorized people shoud have access to information.
## Why it mtters: 
It protects sensitive data from unauthorized access.
## Example:
- Passwords
- Multi-Factor Authentication(MFA)
- Encryption
---
### Integrity
## Definition: 
Information must not be modified without authorization.
## why it matters: 
It ensures data remains accurate and trustworthy.
## Example:
- Hashing
- Digital Signatures.
---
### Availability 
## Definition:
Information and systems should be available when needed. 
## Why it matters:
Users must be able to access systems and data.
## Example:
- Backups
- UPS
- Disaster Recovery
